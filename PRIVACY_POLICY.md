# Privacy Policy — BudgetFlow – Expense Tracker

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Summary

BudgetFlow does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Ever.

---

## What data does BudgetFlow collect?

**Nothing.**

BudgetFlow stores all data exclusively on your device using Android's local storage (SharedPreferences). No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

---

## Data stored locally on your device

The following information is saved **only on your phone**:

- Income sources and amounts
- Fixed expense names and amounts
- Budget category names, allocations, and spending
- Transaction records (amounts, dates, optional notes)
- Savings goals
- Currency preference
- Onboarding completion flag

This data is never transmitted anywhere. It stays on your device until you delete the app or use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings.

---

## Internet access

BudgetFlow does **not** require or use an internet connection. The app works fully offline. No network requests are made.

---

## Analytics & tracking

BudgetFlow contains **no analytics**, **no crash reporting**, **no advertising SDKs**, and **no tracking of any kind**.

---

## Third-party services

BudgetFlow uses **no third-party services**.

---

## Children's privacy

BudgetFlow does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

---

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
